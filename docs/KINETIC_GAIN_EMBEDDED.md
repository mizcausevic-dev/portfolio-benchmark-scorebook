# Kinetic Gain Embedded

Portfolio Benchmark Scorebook is designed to open an embedded executive-intelligence engagement:

- rank portfolio lanes into flagship, expand, prove, and watch tiers using score strength, proof depth, commercial pull, repeatability, and execution drag
- identify which sectors should invest, maintain, watch, or deprioritize before the next board or investor review
- convert a broad portfolio story into one usable scorebook packet with explicit investment and sequencing decisions

This is an operator-facing scorebook-intelligence surface, not legal advice, financial advice, investment advice, or a live market system.
